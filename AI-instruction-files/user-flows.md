# User Flows

## Flow 1 - Shopping on a specific Item

User:
"I need to buy milk"

AI:
- Finds the most affordable milk option based on user's location and preferences.
- Provides a link to purchase the product.

---

## Flow 2 - Conversational Shopping

User:

"I need ingredients for chicken fried rice."

AI:

- Generates shopping list
- Finds products
- Compares stores
- Calculates basket total
- Recommends cheapest option

---

## Flow 3 - Budget Shopping

User:

"I have $100 this week."

AI:

- Reviews historical purchases
- Builds optimized basket
- Suggests cheaper alternatives
